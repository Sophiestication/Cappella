Reporting Issues

If you encounter any bugs or technical issues with CoverSutra, please provide as much detail as possible in your message, including:

  • A description of the issue
  • The steps to reproduce the problem
  • Your operating system version
  • Screenshots, if applicable

This information will help our team diagnose and fix the problem more efficiently.
